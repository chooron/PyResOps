Figure 2. Static, dynamic, and rolling workflows for LLM-assisted reservoir operation. The static workflow executes a full-horizon planning pipeline, the dynamic workflow selects between replan and retain branches at checkpoints, and the rolling workflow triggers LLM-called replanning only when state-risk, forecast-error, or scheduled-check conditions are met. Each workflow produces an auditable decision record with a selected plan, safety status, and evaluation reference.
